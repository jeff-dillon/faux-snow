Find the Good (Enough) Stuff

Ski plans are typically based on natural snowfall totals. The deeper the fresh powder, the better your day will be. There are great sites available that track snow totals and help you make plans - shoutout to opensnow.com.

In the South and Midwest fresh snow is less frequent with less total accumulation. Snow forecasts are therefore less helpful in finding the best conditions. Rather than tracking snowfall, it makes more sense to track the optimal conditions for man-made snow.

Faux / Snow will help find the best man-made powder conditions in the South and Midwest.
